Onsite Classes & Special Events

Blue Mountain Pilates offers private and small-group Pilates sessions at wineries, retreats, corporate gatherings, and special events throughout the Walla Walla Valley.

Each experience is customized to your setting and your group — whether you're planning a wellness-focused retreat, a bridal weekend, or a special celebration that calls for something intentional and energizing.

Equipment and class structure are tailored to the venue and guest experience. All sessions are designed to be welcoming, accessible, and thoughtfully guided.
