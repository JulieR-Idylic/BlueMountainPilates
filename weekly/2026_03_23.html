Greetings Pilates Community!

It can be tempting to think that more movement or more repetitions means more progress. In Pilates, the opposite is often true. Moving slowly with intention is more effective than moving quickly with many repetitions. When we rush, we rely on momentum — that is the Pilates killer! Slow and intended movement is deeply challenging — and far more effective.
