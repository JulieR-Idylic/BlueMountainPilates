Good morning, Pilates friends – I hope daylight saving time is treating you well 😊.

I’m often asked, “How often should I be doing Pilates to start seeing improvement?” or “How do I make this feel less challenging to learn?” The answer is usually the same: consistency.

Some days the work feels natural, and other days it asks more of us. Especially when we’re learning something new, showing up regularly – whether that’s once a week or twice – is what helps the body begin to understand the work.

See you in the studio!
