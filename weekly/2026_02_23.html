Hello Pilates Community!

Consistency doesn’t mean every session feels amazing. It means showing up — especially on the days when it would be easier not to. Some days you’ll feel strong and steady. Other days you’ll feel tight, wobbly, or distracted. That’s normal. The work meets you where you are. We’ll build from there.

  1. No class Friday Feb 27 at 9:30am.
  2. Please join me in giving a warm welcome to Lorrie and Adri! They’ve recently completed their private sessions and are now stepping into semi-privates. This is exactly how the work is meant to evolve — building skill, strength, and community along the way. When you see them in class, say hello and introduce yourself!
  3. Pilates Circuit – Coming Soon! A new Circuit class is nearly here — combining Reformer (including Jumpboard), Mat, Cadillac, Chair, and Spine Corrector into one cohesive, flowing session… with the Ladder Barrel making appearances as we refine the format.

    The goal is to experience how the apparatus complement one another — how support in one place becomes a challenge in another — and how the full system works together. Expect thoughtful transitions, intentional progressions, and a little spark of something new. If this sounds like something you’d enjoy, let me know. I’m gathering interest before finalizing the schedule. It’s possible this format may be transitory like we’ve been offering the Jumpboard — weaving it in over the course of the weeks at various class times.
  4. Text version of these weekly emails: A short list of topics with a link to view the details will go out to all clients who indicated “Text” on their communication preferences. I’m excited to be able to bring that feature to life 😊.
  5. Mat in March – it’s March Matness time! March is all about the Mat. Our Pilates on the Mat classes this month will focus on introducing and building the foundational exercises of the Pilates method. Know someone who’s been Pilates-curious? This is the perfect time for them to jump in. Already love your Reformer sessions? You might be surprised (in a good way!) at how much your Reformer work shows up — and challenges you — on the Mat. 💛 Bring a friend or family member who purchases a Starter Package and receive a $40 referral credit toward your next package. Pilates is even better when it’s shared.
