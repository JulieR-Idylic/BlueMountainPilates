Have you ever noticed that an exercise that once felt manageable suddenly feels much more challenging? That usually isn’t a step backward — it’s a sign that your body is beginning to organize the work differently. As awareness, control, and connection improve, the exercises naturally ask more of the body. In Pilates, the deeper the understanding of the work, the more the work reveals itself.
