Every now and then I hear, “I don’t feel anything.” (And sometimes I see the look that follows… like “uh oh, now I’ve asked for it!” 😄)

In Pilates, the “core” isn’t just about the abdominals working hard — it’s the coordinated support of the entire body. Because of that, the work can feel more subtle than expected. Rather than searching for a strong sensation, begin to take notice of how your body is supporting the movement — your breath, your alignment, your control.

In your next session, shift your focus from feeling more to noticing more — that’s where the real strength develops.
